M4A1-S-RADIANT BEAST

M4A1-S Prism Beast is a VVIP assault rifle variant of the M4A1 Silencer. M4A1-S Prism Beast is a special VVIP weapon based on the M4A1. It has a partial customizable body and the owner can change the color of the various weapon parts. Just like the other M4A1-VIPs, it has fast reloading speed but also features a triple melee attack by holding the RMB.
