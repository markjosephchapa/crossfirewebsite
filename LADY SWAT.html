LADY SWAT

Judging by her name, she is comes from the SWAT division based in Los Angeles, where SWAT was originally established. The GR character wears a light armor suit with dark blue color scheme, similar to SWAT, but it has short shelves, and she wears arms protection with black gloves. BL character sports a more casual outfit with grey/tan color scheme, plus a cap for sunlight protection. She wears a short t-shirt that reveals her bellybutton, plus straps for shotgun shells on her right arm.
