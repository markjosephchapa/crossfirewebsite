QBZ-03-JEWELRY

Similar to M4A1-S Jewelry, it is studded with diamonds, a glass chamber, a magical rose inside and sparkling around the weapon; however, its color background is pink instead of light yellow and there's a doll simply sitting near the iron sight. It has 36 rounds (+1 from VVIP perk) per magazine with faster drawing/reloading speed, even faster than the Digital Camo variant.
