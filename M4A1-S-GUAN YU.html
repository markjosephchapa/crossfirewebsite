M4A1-S-GUAN YU

M4A1-S G Spirit is a VVIP variant of the M4A1 Silencer assault rifle. This VVIP M4A1-S variant features a unique dragon-themed skin with a yellow-red-green color scheme and Guan Yu Spirit decorations. It comes with a 38 round capacity (36 when traded) and overall improved performance stats. This rifle is bundled along with a Glock-18 G Spirit secondary pistol when first bought.
